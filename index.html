About Us

We are a non-governmental organization, empowering a new generation of social entrepreneurs, driving poverty eradication, and catalyzing job creation across Africa.

We bring together young potentials across Africa within a powerful platform to efficiently train, develop, incentivize, and reward feasible endeavors in alignment with the sustainable development goals.

Our Focus

As an organization, our focus is on promoting social entrepreneurship in Africa, particularly among the youth. Our motivation stems from a profound and all-encompassing desire to witness African youths develop and advance using social entrepreneurship.

For us, we empower students and disadvantaged African youth with skills, resources, and support necessary to turn their ideas into successful ventures.

Our Story

Social entrepreneurship has gained popularity among African youth in recent years becoming the backbone of the continent, particularly in fueling innovation, job creation, and economic growth.

Our Solution

With social entrepreneurship programs as our tool and a crucial component, we are focused on encouraging young entrepreneurs in Africa, particularly undergraduates. These programs aim to provide students with the knowledge, skills, resources, and support needed to develop entrepreneurial mindsets thus turning their ideas into successful ventures.

Our programs are tailored to the needs and interests of young Africans, considering factors such as their level of experience, academic background, and available resources. Ultimately, we aim to upscale the rate of employment in Africa and simultaneously transform the economy of the continent.
